# Vision

Enterprises are adopting agents faster than they can define controls. Umbra’s Identity Control Plane becomes the standard layer that answers:

> **Who (human or agent) did what, using which tool, against which resource, and why was it allowed?**

Over time, the platform expands from “allow/deny” into:
- **least-privilege credential brokering** (short-lived scoped credentials),
- **approvals & break-glass** for high-risk actions,
- **tamper-evident receipts** with signature verification,
- deeper enforcement surfaces (HTTP ext_authz, IDE integrations),
- richer policy languages (OPA bundles / relationship models when needed).
